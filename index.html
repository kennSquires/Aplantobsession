A Plant Obsession


Welcome fellow plant lovers, thank you for checking out our page!

A Plant Obsession is a locally owned plant shop located in Lethbridge, Alberta. Our goal as a company is to educate and help our customers with taking care for and buying plants that fit their personal needs. On our website, viewers can find information about A Plant Obsession; the shop goals and personal introductions from us (the owners!) As well as a house plant guide; our idea is to create a cheat book of basic care for tropical house plants. Check out our current guide and keep your eyes open for updates on care for trendy plants!!
