Welcome

First of all, thank you for taking the time to check out our plant shop page, A Plant Obsession! We are a local plant business that is based in Lethbridge, Alberta; we have been running our business from home for about a year. Our main goal as a business is to excite and educate people about house plants, by selling plants, cuttings and providing information with a personal touch; unlike big box stores



With our store just starting out, we are currently just running our shop through social media such as Facebook and Instagram. Our platforms provide plenty of resources for plant lovers such as information on how to care for plants, updates on our collection and current plants we have for sale. With our resources at the moment, we can ship anywhere in Canada; if you are located close to Lethbridge, Alberta we like to personally hand drop off the products.

All our social media platforms for A Plant Obsession will be provided on this page. As well as additional information on us (the owners!!) and our passion for plants!

Kennedy Mitchell

From a young age, plants have always intrigued me whether it was gardening with my grandma or watering my mom’s plants around the house. As I lived on my own, I noticed that I couldn’t help myself and kept bringing a plant home every time I stepped out of the house; my love and collection for plants grew fast. With that love and passion for plants, Tara and I decided to take the step and pursue opening a small business. I am thrilled to meet and help fellow plant lovers with taking care of and buying plants; as well as the opportunity to work with my best friend!

Tara Benfell

My love for plants stemmed from working at a Garden Centre part time, I found the routine of caring for the plants calming and grounding. While working at the garden center I had the ability to learn so much about the varieties of different plants and environmental conditions for plants to thrive. Although one of my favorite aspects of plants is the interior design element they have when it comes to designing; plants can make the home feel so light and tropical, while being a statement piece! I really look forward to pursuing a career in building a plant shop that provides a welcoming, helping hand for plant lovers!
